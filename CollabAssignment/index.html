Collaboration

"The action of working together with someone to produce or create something."

How To: Collaboration in Business

First create openness and transparency about company goals and coworkers. Make sure to establish a judgement free idea sharing office. Encourage meetings between departments, don’t limit collaboration to just a single department. Lead by example, being an open and collaborative leader will allow you employees feel more comfortable to share. Lastly, offer positivity and rewards to good collaborative practices.

Why is Collaboration Important to Business?

Collaboration in the workplace helps create an open environment where people feel valued and heard.

This is also an opportunity to have workers learn from each other, collaboration requires viewpoints and feedback, no two people have the same idea.

Adding more people allows a more diverse skills for a team and also adds more perspectives to the project.

Working in teams can have more impact than a singular person, this adds more knowledge and efficiency.
